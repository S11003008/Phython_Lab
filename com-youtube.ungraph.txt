<h1>Youtube social network and ground-truth communities</h1>

<h3>Dataset information</h3>

<p>
<a href="http://www.youtube.com">Youtube</a> is a video-sharing web site that includes a social network. 
In the Youtube social network, users form friendship each other and users can create groups which other users can join.
We consider such user-defined groups as ground-truth communities. 
This data is provided by <a href="http://socialnetworks.mpi-sws.org/data-imc2007.html">Alan Mislove et al.</a>
</p>

<p>
We regard each connected component in a group as a separate ground-truth community. We remove the ground-truth communities which have less than 3 nodes.
We also provide the top 5,000 communities with highest quality which are described in <a href="http://arxiv.org/abs/1205.6233">our paper</a>.
As for the network, we provide the largest connected component.
</p>
